Chris Zhu? Chris who?

I have a digitally-focused passion in making more with less; using motion, interaction, and visual design to define campaigns across a variety of causes and campaigns. I bring an extroverted brand of teamplay to any project.


I received my degree in Visual Communication from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago, falling madly in love with the city. Since then, I've worked with more than a few local institutions, such as the Obama Foundation, Chicago Sun-Times, and the Art Institute of Chicago.


For years I've also volunteered in creative or organizing capacities for health causes such as: the Chicago Department of Public Health, the Multiple Sclerosis Assocation of America, and Howard Brown Health. Combating complex issues for the wellbeing of the community is a passion for me.
