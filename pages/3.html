Women Who Make America is a docuseries featuring contemporary female leaders in fields like art, science, and entrepreneurship. I was challenged to create key animations that could be applied to a pilot episode and future endeavors. I developed a punk-feminist motion identity to frame the achievements of these trailblazers!
