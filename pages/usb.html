Designing UX to scale for 500K users at U.S. Bank

Client
U.S. Bank
Role
Product Design Lead
Project Timespan
Sep 2022–Oct 2024
In 2021, U.S. Bank acquired the fintech startup Bento for Business, to adapt their corporate card app into a much more complex credit servicing and spend management platform.

I was brought on to help scale the app to U.S. Bank's 500K small-business customer base, reimagining dozens of flows for much more diverse use cases. This was a massive project in scale, ambition, and volume.
