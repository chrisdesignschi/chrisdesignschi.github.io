Albany Park

Date Completed
2022
Role
Sr. Visual Designer
Tools used
Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator, Paper/Pen
Albany Park was looking to replatform to Shopify for their furniture retail site, and was in serious need of a visual refresh to accompany their move to a new platform.

The brand was looking for a design partner to help them understand what was working well, what wasn't, and craft an eCommerce platform that combined their powerful grassroots brand with a best-in-class user experience.
