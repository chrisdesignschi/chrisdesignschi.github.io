Albany Park is a pandemic success story. A potent combination of trendy designs, affordable prices, and fast home delivery led to a surge in furniture sales in 2020.

To extend reach to new customers, the brand aimed to modernize their e-commerce platform. I partnered with Albany Park to create a best-in-class user experience, and deliver the promise of convenient luxury to customers.

SOLUTION

The brand had no problem getting users to visit the site and view products, but had trouble converting that attention to sales—due to several bottlenecks in the customer experience.

I identified areas in the sales funnel that could be refined or removed, giving potential customers all the information they needed to quickly move from cart to checkout.

takeaways

Online shoppers are smart! They know how to quickly sift through lots of information to find what they want.

Refining Albany Park’s customer UX was an exercise in simplifying to strengthen: providing easy access to the information customers need in order to make a decision, while removing processes that weren’t working.
