After a year of pandemic-induced staff shortages, hospital staff (especially nurses) were in historically high demand. So we helped HCA step in, creating tools for them to retain and grow their staff. We took to social and video, creating a comprehensive campaign to appreciate and recognize the heroes that make HCA.

starting with snapchat

We pitched and delivered a novel way to modernize and reactivate HCA's image among young professionals with a series of Snapchat Geofilters. Geofilters are a Snapchat feature that activate when a user is posting something nearby a target, like an HCA hospital.
