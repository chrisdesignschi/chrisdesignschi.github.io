Client
NorthShore
Role
Sr. Visual Designer
Tools Used

During the pandemic, telefitness emerged as a safe way to help physical therapy patients recover at home.

I took the lead on designing NorthShore Connect as an effective method to connect users to their physical therapists. The app helps build healthy habits for injury recovery, with guided feedback from your provider.
