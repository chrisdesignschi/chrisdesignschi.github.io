#PRODUCT DESIGN - 2019

Mosaic — Democratized Social Media Concept

Interface DesignSocial Media


Mosaic is a social media platform concept that I developed with the intent of focusing on user creation over consumption. This video highlights how the platforms encourages users to be creative in the way that they interact with others, collaborating in a way that reflects their own creative nuances.

#VISUAL DESIGN - 2019

Volkswagen Auto Diagnostics App

Interface DesignExperience Design


The Volkswagen Auto Diagnostics app is a new partner for an old friend—using the diagnostic heuristics in John Muir's How to Keep your Volkswagen Alive, I designed an easy experience for identifying and fixing the mechanical problems with your vintage VW vehicle. You can be reminded of checkup times, schedule diagnostics, and easily root out problems.

#DATA DESIGN - 2019

2018 Myfonts Customer Survey

The Myfonts Font Purchasing survey is an annual poll conducted by Myfonts to understand trends in the design market. Who is buying fonts, and what kinds? I assembled this report to show statistics like levels of experience as well as overall font useage among designers.
