#DATA VIZ - 2020

Visualizing the Opioid Crisis through epidemiological data for policymakers

Public Health Data Visualization

The opioid crisis in Chicago is a complex and growing problem.


In collaboration with the Chicago Department of Public Health, I've led volunteers in raising awareness on the issue and training civic partners for 2 years. One of my main responsibilities was creating a report to help the department to communicate the severity of the crisis for policymakers. Advocating for positive change in public health becomes easier when the facts are readily available and well visualized!

GoalsVisualize epidemiology data in an effective and incisive manner to help public health experts advocate for better policy
