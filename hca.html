Expanding HCA's Social Media Presence in 2020

Social Media Healthcare

HCA Healthcare is the nation's leading provider of healthcare services across 184 hospitals, and 2,000 sites of care.


I art directed a holistic ad campaign for HCA Healthcare, ranging from social videos to new solutions such as Snapchat Geofilters that raised HCA's name-brand amongst new nurses and other professionals.



GoalsEngage a younger audience with HCA Healthcare social media posts, especially current and/or potential nurses.

Snapchat Geofilters


I pitched and delivered a novel way to lighten up HCA's reputation among young professionals—especially nurses—with a series of Snapchat Geofilters. Geofilters are a Snapchat feature that activate when a user is posting something nearby a target, like an HCA hospital.

The look and feel of these remains in the HCA visual brand, but adopts a more modern and fun look fitting of organic social media.

Process


Here's an example of the some of the groundwork I did in making these events come alive for HCA. A difficulty with the process was a need to stick to HCA Healthcare Brand guidelines; which visually, would not play well on social media or with the target audience (younger professionals).

Thus, I had the idea to use calligraphy and drawings to sell the HCA aesthetic without creating compromising visual elements.

Social Videos

I created a suite of three video strategies for HCA's primary social media platforms: Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn.

Instagram: Create content highlighting young professionals who work at HCA Hospitals, or may be interested in doing so (HCA employs over 280,000 workers in the healthcare field.)

Facebook: Establish trust and reputation with HCA's accomplishments especially during the pandemic.

LinkedIn: Attract high-demand positions to local HCA hospitals using the power of their national brand.
